About

Tim Grange

I started a 5yr apprenticeship in 1975 at Vidal Sassoon Bond street London, I passed a colouring and Perming diploma at loreal School, Bruton Street London, I worked for Vidal Sassoon for 8yrs until moving to Suffolk where I worked as a manager for Robert Owen Haircutters and also trained apprentices to national standard, I worked there for 12yrs until moving to Norfolk.

Erika Jade

I started my beauty career in 2009. I completed 2 years at city college norwich studying NVQ2 + NVQ3 in beauty therapy. In 2010 I won an award for outstanding progression in the beauty industry. I have since further completed indian head massage, spray tanning and in 2016 done some further traning in calgel nails.
